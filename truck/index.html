For Sale

2007 Classic Chevrolet Silverado 2500HD Diesel

Up for sale is my 2007 Classic Chevrolet Silverado 2500HD, with the LBZ Duramax Diesel. It is a single cab, long bed truck, 2 wheel drive. This truck is a pre-emissions diesel, which means it has no DPF filter or any of the associated problematic hardware. I've owned this truck since August of 2020, when I purchased it from the city of Henderson, Nevada. As far as I can tell, prior to my ownership it was a maintenance vehicle and never actually towed a trailer. In my ownership I've used it to tow a small U-Haul trailer around town a handful of times, and it has spent the majority of my ownership as a garage queen.

Mechanically, the truck is 100% stock, and has never been modified or tuned. The only modifications I made were for creature comforts on the inside, which included swapping in better seats and a slightly nicer interior. If you're familiar with the GMT800 trucks, it was most of the way from a W/T spec to something closer to an LS trim level.

I purchased the truck with 136,079 miles, and as it currently sits, it has 139,350. Upon purchase, I went from bumper to bumper on the truck and changed every fluid and filter, including engine oil (and filter), transmission fluid (and both the internal and external filter), fuel filter, air filter, rear differential fluid. I also purchased new OEM AC Delco brake pads front and rear, but discovered the truck didn't need them. The full exhaustive list of fixes and upgrades are below.

Why are you selling it?
